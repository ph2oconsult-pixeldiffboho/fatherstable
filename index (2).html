Common Questions

What do I get with Full Access? ▼
Full Access unlocks the complete library of 40+ Bible stories, all printable workbooks and activities (colouring pages, word searches, journals, memory verse cards), the full 12-month curriculum, and the Read Aloud narration feature.
Can I cancel at any time? ▼
Yes, absolutely. There are no lock-in contracts. You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings, and you'll retain access until the end of your billing period.
Is my payment information secure? ▼
Yes. All payments are processed securely by PayPal, one of the world's most trusted and recognised payment platforms. You can pay with your PayPal balance or any major credit or debit card. Anchor & Oak never stores your payment details.
How many children can I use this with? ▼
One subscription covers your whole family. Use it with as many children as you have at home — there are no per-child fees.
What is the annual plan? ▼
The annual plan gives you 12 months of Full Access for the price of around 7 months — a 40% saving compared to paying monthly. It's billed once per year.
